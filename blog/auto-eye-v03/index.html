Auto Eye v03

- May 21, 2020


The Auto Eye v03 are officially released!


Here are some renders that I did for the new release. All rendered in Cycles, but it works on Eevee too.


A lot of improvements were done to the add-on, texture/material improvements and improved baked, now you can bake color, specular, roughness and bump maps. This new version works on Blender 2.81, 2.82 and will be updated if needed for the next versions.
