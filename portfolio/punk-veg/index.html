Going to the Street Market

Category: Personal Work
Software: Blender


This work is inspired in something that I enjoy to do a lot, going the street market buy and eat food, vegan food of course.


I’ve used some new great add-ons on this illustration that helped me to speed up the process and worth mention as a thanks for the devs.


  • Hair Extension Toolkit
  • Quad Remesher


This project was done using Blender only, render in Cycles. And as always I’ve used my add-on Auto Eye too.


Hope you like it and feel free to ask anything.
